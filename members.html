Shengbiao Wu, Ph.D.

Research Assistant Professor

Email: shengwu@hku.hk

Google Scholar | ResearchGate | Twitter

Dr. Shengbiao Wu is a Research Assistant Professor in the Division of Landscape Architecture at the University of Hong Kong. He received the B.S. degree in base class of geography at Wuhan University in 2013, and the Ph.D. degree in Cartography and Geography Information System at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in June 2019.


His research interests include the physics of remote sensing, radiative transfer modelling, vegetation phenology monitoring, urban surface energy budget, and machine learning in urban ecology studies.
